Introduction

Welcome to our comprehensive guide on solving the Rubik's Cube. Whether you are a beginner looking to solve the cube for the first time, or an experienced solver aiming to improve your solving speed, this guide will provide you with the necessary steps and techniques.



Step 1: Solve the White Cross

To start, we need to solve the white cross. The goal is to align the white edge pieces with the center pieces on all four sides. Here’s how:

  • Find a white edge piece and move it to the correct position on the white face.
  • Repeat this for the remaining white edge pieces, ensuring they align with the corresponding center pieces on the adjacent faces.

Step 2: Solve the White Corners

Next, we need to solve the white corners. The objective is to position the white corner pieces in their correct spots between the white center and the adjacent edge pieces. Here’s how:

  • Locate a white corner piece in the bottom layer.
  • Rotate the bottom layer until the corner piece is directly below its intended position.
  • Use a specific algorithm (R U R' U') to place the corner in the correct position.

Step 3: Solve the Middle Layer Edges

Now, we need to solve the middle layer edges. The goal is to place the edge pieces of the middle layer in their correct positions. Here’s how:

  • Find an edge piece in the top layer that does not contain yellow.
  • Rotate the top layer until the edge piece is directly above its intended position in the middle layer.
  • Use the appropriate algorithm (U R U' R' U' F' U F for right placement or U' L' U L U F U' F' for left placement) to position the edge piece correctly.

Step 4: Solve the Yellow Cross

Next, we need to solve the yellow cross. The goal is to create a yellow cross on the top face. Here’s how:

  • Position the yellow edges using the F R U R' U' F' algorithm. Repeat this until you have a yellow cross on the top face.
  • Ensure the yellow edge pieces align with the center pieces on the sides.

Step 5: Solve the Yellow Corners

Finally, we need to solve the yellow corners. The goal is to position and orient the yellow corners correctly. Here’s how:

  • Position the yellow corners using the algorithm (U R U' L' U R' U' L) to move them to their correct positions.
  • Orient the yellow corners using the algorithm (R U R' U R U2 R') until all the yellow corners are correctly oriented.
